Required Documents

  • Birth Certificate (Original + Copy)
  • Previous School's Transfer Certificate
  • Report Card of Last Class Attended
  • 6 Passport Size Photographs of Student
  • Aadhar Card of Student & Parents
  • Caste Certificate (if applicable)
